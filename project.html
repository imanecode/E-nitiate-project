What is volun-App?

VolunApp is a desktop application created by moroccan high school student, to promote community service by offering a space where organisations can share their needs,And where volunteers can find the program fitting their skills and calendar!


Why do we need you to volunteers?

With small actions, you can completely change your community,make it better for us and generations to come. Goals like ending war and hunger might sound utopic to you,But the truth is that volunteering will bring a world of rewards you might never have expected. In addition to that,you have to know that volunteering is not only about the change you will make in your community,but also the change you will see in yourself. since community service helps you acquire various skills,such as: time management,leadership,and flexibility etc..

How to use VolunApp?

For volunteers:

Select the fields of your interest to display organizations

click the ">>" box to know more about the organization

contact the organization

For organisations:

Enter the required informations

submit and wait for volunteers to contact you

Download


head to github website and download our script from it , make sure to have graphics.py lib along side with the file or already in your library
